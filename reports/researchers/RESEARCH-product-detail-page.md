## Research Report: Product Detail Page (PDP)

### Executive Summary
The Product Detail Page (PDP) is critical for conversion, requiring a balance between high-quality visual assets, clear product information, prominent calls-to-action (CTA), and social proof. Best practices dictate a clean layout, mobile-first responsiveness, and minimizing friction for the "Add to Cart" action.

### Findings

#### Finding 1: Visual Dominance and Galleries
High-quality imagery is the most critical element of a PDP. Users heavily rely on product photos to make purchasing decisions.
- Source: [NN/g - Presenting Product Images](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/product-images/)
- Confidence: High

#### Finding 2: Prominent, Persistent CTA
The "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now" button should be the most prominent element on the page, positioned above the fold on desktop and preferably sticky on mobile.
- Source: [Smashing Magazine - Designing Product Pages](https://www.smashingmagazine.com/)
- Confidence: High

#### Finding 3: Clear Hierarchy of Information
Information must be structured logically: Title, Price, Ratings -> Key Features/Variations -> CTA -> Detailed Description -> Reviews/Social Proof.
- Source: [Baymard Institute - Product Details Page UX](https://baymard.com/)
- Confidence: High

#### Finding 4: Minimizing Friction and Addressing Doubts
Including shipping costs, return policies, and availability (in-stock status) near the CTA significantly decreases cart abandonment.
- Source: [Baymard Institute - Shipping Costs](https://baymard.com/)
- Confidence: High

### Recommendations
1. **Recommended**: Implement a large image gallery standard to the left (desktop) or top (mobile) because visual validation is paramount.
2. **Recommended**: Utilize a sticky footer or header for the CTA on mobile because it keeps the primary conversion action always accessible.
3. **Recommended**: Structure the right column (or beneath the image on mobile) with clear typography: Title (H1), distinct Price, selectable variants, and a high-contrast CTA button.
